How it works
Skill Rating weighs Strokes Gained components by their regression impact on four result targets (SG Total, top-10s, money, FedExCup rank). Outcome Elo converts each season's results into a multi-player Elo update against the field. Skill looks forward; Elo looks back. All metrics are standardized within season, so 2016 and 2025 are directly comparable.
